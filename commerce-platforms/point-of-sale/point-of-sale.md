## Unified commerce

All-in-one offering for successful online to in-store payment experiences

HiPay, as your preferential partner for unified commerce, provides a scalable offer for efficient payment experiences based on a platform which centralizes all your online and in-store payments in one single interface.

It relies on three main pillars:
- A Scalable & Unified platform that evolves depending on your needs
- From Online to In-store to help with all purchasing experiences
- For Successful Payments thanks to a set of complementary solutions

## In-store payment systems

In-store payments can be processed with two different payment systems:
- Centralized : all your transactions are centralized on the servers of the payment gateway
- Standalone : all your transactions are directly stored in your POS payment terminals

In HiPay, our solutions are compatible with both payment systems to fit your needs and preferences.
